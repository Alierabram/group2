Message from the Head of Department

Welcome to the Department of Computing & Informatics at Bugema University. Our department is committed to delivering high-quality education, practical experience and research that address real-world challenges. We emphasize strong theoretical foundations, hands-on lab work, ethical practice and community engagement.

Our graduates are prepared to excel in industry, academia and public service. We invite you to explore our programs and join us in shaping the future through computing.

Mr. Ssewankambo Erma

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, practice-oriented computing education and research that empowers students to solve societal problems and drive technological innovation.

  • Deliver up-to-date curricula aligned with industry needs
  • Promote research and innovation
  • Support ethical and community-centered computing

Our Vision

To be a leading centre of excellence in computing education and research in the region, producing transformative leaders in technology and information sciences.
